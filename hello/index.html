Good to see you here. I'm not really sure how did you find your way to this link, but I'm really glad you did. I'm Manish Ahuja, and CogentDesi is an online alias, that I've given myself.

So, hang on tight and fasten your seat-belts. I'm excited and nervous about this journey. Hope to see you make a visit every time I've something new to share. Looking forward to your thoughts on what you see/ read in this space.
